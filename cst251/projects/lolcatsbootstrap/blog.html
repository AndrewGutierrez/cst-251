ABOUT THE AUTHOR:
Catleen Bengal dropped the "h" from her first name when she entered the second grade. Her teacher asked her not to come to school with her two favorite cats (she had eight at the time) tucked in her oversized lunch bag and she was soooooo upset, she changed her name in protest. Catleen has continued her quest for the purrrfect life in New York City where she shares a 450 square foot studio apartment with her seventeen furry BFFs.
